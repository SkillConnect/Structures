STRUCTURAL AUDIT

Structural Audit is a preliminary technical survey of a building to assess its general health as a civil engineering structure. It is usually initiated as the first step for repair. These audits are a government mandate for every structure as it ages.

Some of the main tasks that we do as a part of structural audit:
  • Rapid Visual Screening of Buildings
  • Structural General Arrangement Drawings Preparation
