At STRUCTURES, we provide Structural Engineering services to architects, owners, contractors, and developers.

Since our establishment in 1993 as a Associateship practice by Er. Milind Rathi, we have designed countless landmarks with projects spread across myriad geographies and environments. Thereby establishing our reputation for excellence in design and the ability to respond quickly to the Customized demands of every project.

With our offices located at Jalgaon, Nasik, and Pune, Structures synergizes its strengths in technical expertise, cutting-edge software modeling, and analysis tools, along with the best talent, to deliver innovative and technically sound yet safe, Durable, and optimal structural design solutions to our clients.
