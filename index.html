    At STRUCTURES, we provide services to architects, owners, contractors, and developers.

    Since our establishment in 1993 as a proprietary Associateship practice by Er. Milind Rathi, we have designed countless landmarks with projects spread across myriad geographies and environments.

    Thereby establishing our reputation for excellence in design and the ability to respond quickly to the Customized demands of every project.
